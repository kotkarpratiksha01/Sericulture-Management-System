About Sericulture in Sangamner

Sangamner, located in Ahmednagar district, is one of the important hubs of sericulture in Maharashtra. Farmers here cultivate mulberry and rear silkworms for high-quality cocoons and eggs. The favorable climate and farming practices make Sangamner a reliable source of silk products.
